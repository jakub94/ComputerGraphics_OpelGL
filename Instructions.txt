Right Mouse Button: Generate Cube on Cursor Position
Right Mouse Button + Shift: Generate Pyramide on Cursor Position

Left Mouse Button: Select and deselect Cube

Space: Delete all Cubes
Space + Shift: Delete all Pyramides

W / A / S / D: Move selected cubes around
Arrow Keys: Rotate selected cubes Cube

Press and hold "+ Key": increase Rotation Speed of Cubes
Press and hold "- Key": decrease Rotation Speed of Cubes
Press and hold "+ Key" + Shift : increase Moving Speed of Cubes
Press and hold "- Key" + Shift : decrease Moving Speed of Cubes

1 / 2 / 3 Buttons: Toggle Pyramid Rotation axis (on / off) // Before Creating Pyramid
	1 --> x
	2 --> y
	3 --> z
